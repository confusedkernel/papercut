papercut includes a lightweight client-side search page and section-aware navigation.

## Header search

Desktop search starts as an icon and expands into an input. Mobile search is inside the menu.

## Taxonomy navigation

- Terms pages show taxonomy buttons with post counts.
- Term detail pages show breadcrumbs and matching post lists.

## Post navigation

Single post pages can show previous and next links below the content card.
